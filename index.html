What we do?

The food that hasn't been consumed will be given to those who deserve it. We link donors and NGO's immediately. We will offer the service to a number of supermarkets so that they can give away their goods before they expire.

Why we do?

Nowadays, food is wasted in unacceptably large amounts. Because of this, a lot of people, particularly minors, pass away from hunger. Therefore, we are putting this together in an effort to reduce food waste and aid those in need.
